About us

Anjalai Ammal Mahalingam Engineering College (AAMEC) stands, as a beacon light in brick and mortar,men and materials, involved in learning and teaching of Technical education.

The College is located in a sprawling campus of about 65 acres of land at the historic site of Kovilvenni, The Thanjavur to Nagapattinam National Highway(NH 67) runs in front of the college and buses on this road are quite frequent.
